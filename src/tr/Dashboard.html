A Topluluk Platform

Accessible Çince learning for İspanyolca speakers

The Mission

PandaLatam by ODL was born with the goal of creating quality resources to learn Çince tailored specifically for the İspanyolca-speaking community. Language should not be a barrier to connecting cultures.

How the Platform Works

Learning Seviyes

Dersler structured by levels (A1 to C1) with audio support, translations and dynamic iteration.

Topluluk

Explore PDF books and lessons created by other users, or contribute by sharing your own!

Word of the Day (AI)

Daily interactive widget powered by Artificial Intelligence with pronunciation, examples and quick quizzes.

AI Assistant

Smart chat integrated that understands your level and solves your Çince doubts in real time.

Multiplatform

Learn wherever you are by installing our PWA. Full support for automatic dark mode.

Reliable Grammar

Dictionaries, validated platforms like Quizlet and suggested classes with native teachers on italki.
